Playing Favorites is a simple application that lets SoundCloud users quickly browse through their favorites, tracks 'favorited' be their followers, and tracks 'favorited' by users they are following. All you need to play is a SoundCloud account. The more users you connect with, and the more tracks you like, the more fun the application becomes.
